About us


We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making information about the environment simple and available to everyone. The organization has developed from a small circle of friends and is constantly evolving. The easiest and fastest way to reach people without wasting resources is the Internet. Therefore we decided to create this homepage. It should serve us as a mouthpiece to make simple and compact information accessible to as many people as possible.


Because the environment concerns us all!
